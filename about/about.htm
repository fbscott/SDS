About

For Jackie, home staging isn't just a career… it's a life dream. After graduating with a degree in Interior Design and emphasis in Art, Jackie quickly jumped into the design world. Over the last decade, Jackie has experienced all aspects of build/stage design, worked as an assistant designer for professional Interior Designers, and served as head designer for several custom home builders. Jackie knows her way around properties and has landed in a field which gives her great pleasure and joy as she uses her creativity and talent to impress her customers. Starting her own company allows her to invest all her energy and focus on not just pleasing work for herself, but impressing and satisfying others as well. Outside of work, Jackie spends her free time with her husband and three kids, where she loves cooking, traveling and outdoor recreational activities. Her love of fitness and family also provide creative ways to express herself.
