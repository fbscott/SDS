About

For Jackie, home staging "isn't just a career… it's a lifetime dream." With her BA degree in Interior Design and Art emphasis, Jackie easily stepped into the design world, and now has 10+ years of experience to freelance and share with home owners and realtors. Over the last decade, she has accumulated experience in all aspects of custom and new home construction and design, renovations as well as staging for home sellers or special events. She has been an assistant designer for professional Interior Designers, and served as lead designer for several custom home builders. Jackie knows her way around properties and has excelled in a field which gives her great satisfaction as she uses her creativity and talent to bring to life the dreams of her clients. Sanders Design Stage allows her to invest all her design creativity, energy and focus on the complete satisfaction of her customers whether they are planning a special event, selling a home or making a difference in the one they are living in. She has done it all.

Outside of work Jackie spends her free time with her husband and three children who love camping and outdoor recreation. International travel with her husband is high on her priority list. She is proud to have eaten in all 4 food meccas of America and is a gourmand who loves to entertain family and friends. Redesigning their living space is another one of her passions.
