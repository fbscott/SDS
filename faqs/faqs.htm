Q:

As a realtor, why do I need a professional stager?

A:

Homes going on the market should be shown in their best condition. As an agent, your sole focus is selling the home. As a home stager, my job is getting that home in prime condition and pointing out problem areas that may ward off potential buyers. You'd be surprised at how seemingly small details can affect a buyer's opinion of the home. Cluttered spaces, crowded rooms, and dirty windows or carpet can turn off many buyers. Additionally, having a neutral third party, such as a stager, keeps the relationship between home owner and agent focused and friendly, and results in a beautifully showcased home!

Q:

What types of homes should be staged?

A:

Every single home – large and small – will benefit from staging. Additional accessories, furniture, art can always be added to spruce up a space, or just cleaning up areas to get them picture ready can be so rewarding for the home owner and appealing for buyers.

Q:

What time commitment is required for a home to be staged?

A:

This depends on the condition and size of the home. An initial walk though will take place to determine exact hours needed. Let's get together and talk!
