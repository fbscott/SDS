Services

Please review the options below that best suit your needs and comfort level, and contact today for your in-home consolation. Looking forward to doing business with you.

Occupied Home Staging

Using the clients existing furnishings, art and décor to create a fresh, appealing look to get your home market ready.

Mixed Home Staging

Often times a client has moved out pieces but has some that could be incorporated into the finished staging. Jackie uses a local warehouse which specializes in home staging furniture/accessories to rent, for a specified period of time (at a fee charged to the client) to add to existing possessions and includes anything imaginable that may be missing from the finished stage.

Vacant Home Staging

After space planning, furniture layout and design, a proposal will be compiled and presented which includes everything a lived in home has to offer. Rugs, art, accessories, dishes… all will be rented for said period of time (at a fee charged to the client) from a local warehouse, who specializes in said products.

Seasonal & Event Decorating

Includes but not limited to weddings, holidays and social parties.

Design

Color consultations, flooring options, furniture and décor placement…Lets talk!

Re-Design

Creating a budget that works for you and then shopping for, purchasing décor, accessories, lighting, furniture for your new space.

No matter your homes current state, all can benefit from at minimum, a staging consultation. A professional and trained set of eyes will greatly increase the likelihood of your home listing and selling for market value or above, and Sanders Design Staging is the company to take you there.

Additionally, many home stagers overlook dirty windows, or spotted carpet. However, Jackie not only pays attention to small details (which to a buyer make a large difference) but has connections with roofers, painters, carpenters, glass work, you name it. Truly a one stop shop.
