Services

No matter what condition your home is currently in, all homes will benefit from a staging consultation. A professional and trained set of eyes will greatly increase the likelihood of your home listing and selling for market value or above, and Sanders Design Staging is the company to take you there.

Occupied Home Staging

Getting a home ready to be sold or just to freshen the space.

An in-home consultation – typically done with the homeowner – where we walk through your home and identify ways to improve its appeal. If it's a quick fix, such as de-cluttering or rearranging furniture, we'll take care of it on the spot. Some homeowners may prefer to use their existing furnishings and décor. Others may choose to lease additional furnishings that complement the look and design of your home. Whatever your desire and budget, I can identify ways to improve your home's appeal!

Mixed Home Staging

Often times a client has moved out pieces but has some that could be incorporated into the finished staging. Jackie uses a local warehouse which specializes in home staging furniture/accessories to rent, for a specified period of time (at a fee charged to the client) to add to existing possessions and includes anything imaginable that may be missing from the finished stage.

Vacant Home Staging

For a vacant home going on the marker, or home otherwise not occupied.

Potential home buyers often need assistance visualizing how a vacant house can be a home. Using the home's natural appeal and leased furnishings, I will create a warm and homey feeling that will appeal to potential buyers.

Seasonal & Event Decorating

Includes but not limited to weddings, holidays and social parties. Whatever your needs are, let's get together and see how I can be of service to you!

Design & Re-Design

Whether you are selling your home or fixing up your space for your enjoyment, design and re-design are custom fit for your needs. Color consultations, flooring options, furniture and décor placement… are all part of this process.

Working directly with you and discussing problem areas or rooms that need improving will take place during an initial consultation, as well as determining your style preferences, budget requirements and overall fished look that is desired. The sky is the limit. Lets talk!
