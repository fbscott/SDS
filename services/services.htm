Services

A staged home will generate more web traffic and foot traffic! Additionally buyers are more inclined to view and purchase a home that shows-well and looks cared for. Professional and high-quality staging is the way to achieve this, with little or no effort from the homeowner. Using a professional stager, the home will be more attractive to prospective buyers, and result in a shorter time on the market and quicker turn over which is the ultimate goal.

Not everything to improve staging a home is about furniture or art. Professionally-trained "eyes" see things to be corrected that could be a deal breaker for potential buyers. Once identified by the processional, they can be corrected by the homeowner or the team of experts who are qualified to make repairs. SDS will connect you with experts from roofing to carpet cleaners to endure that the home is at its best.

Consider your options and select one that best suits your needs and budge. SDS will make your home show best whether you are selling or looking for some fresh ideas from a designer.

No matter what condition your home is currently in, it will benefit from a staging consultation. A professional and trained set of eyes will greatly increase the likelihood of your home selling quickly for market value or above, and Sanders Design Stage is the company to take you there.

Occupied Home Staging

Are you realtor looking to listing a home, a home owner looking to "freshen up a space?"

An in-home consultation will identify ways to improve your home and increase its appeal. If it's a quick fix, such as de-cluttering or rearranging furniture, Sanders Design Stage will take care of it on the spot. Some homeowners may prefer to use their existing furnishings and décor. Others may choose to lease additional furnishings that complement the look and design of the home. Whatever your desire and budget, SDS can identify ways to improve your home's appeal!

Vacant Home Staging

For a vacant home going on the marker, or home soon to be empty.

Potential home buyers often need assistance visualizing how the space in a vacant house can best function. Using the home's natural appeal and architecture with incorporating some leased furnishings, SDS will create a warm and inviting atmosphere that will appeal to potential buyers.

Seasonal & Event Decorating

Includes but not limited to weddings, holidays and special occasion events. Whatever your needs are, let's get together to discuss how SDS can be of service to you!

Design & Re-Design

Whether you are selling your home or fixing up your space for your enjoyment, custom design and re-design will meet your needs. Color consultations, flooring options, furniture and décor placement… are all part of this process.

Working directly with you and discussing problem areas or rooms that need improving will take place during an initial consultation, and will reveal your style preferences, budget requirements and overall fished look that is desired. Let's talk!
